**ABOUT THIS PROJECT**

This game involves you waking up in a facility that you have to escape from. In the facility, there are bodyguards roaming around ready to attack you. To fight them, you must usethe objects lying around to your advantage
to fight the bodyguards off. I took some inspiration from the _Metroid_ and _Metal Gear_ series when making this game. 

One thing I worked on was developing background music with GarageBand. I tried to give the song an upbeat feel to keep the player consistently engaged with the game.
