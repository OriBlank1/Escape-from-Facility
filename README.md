**ABOUT THIS PROJECT**

This game involves you waking up in a facility that you have to escape from. In the facility, there are bodyguards roaming around ready to attack you. To fight them, you must usethe objects lying around to your advantage to fight the bodyguards off. I took some inspiration from the _Metroid_ and _Metal Gear_ series when making this game. 

One thing I worked on was developing background music with GarageBand. I tried to give the song an upbeat feel to keep the player consistently engaged with the game. I dragged the song as an mp3 file onto my player asset and checked loop so it would play over and over again. 

<img width="2556" height="1179" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7104418e-9546-4d63-a82d-0afa39960c84" />

Another thing I worked on was using Google Gemini to help me with programming the behavior of the bodyguards. I wanted to give the bodyguards mobility to attack you as if they were a common enemy in a video game. This was probably the hardest part as I needed to make sure the bodyguards were only following me when they saw me, they didn't push me out of the level, and they didn't appear above or below the plane. Additionally, I had to cooperate with a baked navigation mesh for my level so that the bodyguards knew where they could walk. I fixed this by making sure they were tracking the main camera as a reference and that they would be destroyed by a weapon that had the tag "Holdable".
